Dark/Light Mode Image Generator

This creates an image where, you see one image when you look at it in dark mode, and you see a different image when you look at it in light mode!

For the best results, upload two images with the same dimensions.


To see it in action, save the image then upload it to Discord. Then, the image will change depending on whether you are in dark mode or light mode!
